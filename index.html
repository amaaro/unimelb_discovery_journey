Our team provides an extensive range of intellectual property and commercialisation support services to the University and its researchers. Specifically we:

  • Identify, develop and commercialise the Universityâ€™s intellectual property assets.
    • We play a role in identifying prospective intellectual property and inventions that have commercial potential and lead the shaping and protection of these intellectual assets
    • We work with the researchers and business development teams to increase the commercial attractiveness of the intellectual assets and market these to identify opportunities for achieving adoption and impact
    • We assist in raising funds to develop and commercialise the intellectual assets
    • We lead the engagement with the University’s venture capital partners, Uniseed and IP Group, as well as other investors
    • We are experts in licensing and start-ups and play a lead role in these activities
    • We are experts in IP and work with Business Development, Partnerships, Legal and Contracts on complex transactions ensuring suitable IP arrangements and commercial terms
    • We take an active role in growing the University’s technology transfer and commercialisation capabilities, including providing education programs to the researchers.
  • Manage the technology pipeline, equity and licensed asset portfolios.
    • We are the custodian of the University’s technology pipeline and seek to grow and develop a strong pipeline to further the University's research and impact goals
    • We manage the University’s patent portfolio
    • We manage the University’s equity portfolio and its portfolio of licensed assets to generate benefits and returns
    • We manage the flow of royalties and financial returns to inventors
  • Own the startup and licensing processes ensuring compliance to the University statute.
    • We are the delegated authority to sign off on licensing and start-up deals that go to the University decision-maker(s)

Discovery Journey

Welcome.

Whether you need to present to potential industry partners, research institutions, or investors for their support, we invite you on this journey of discovery to learn about yourself and the key knowledge and skills you will need to build and deliver a highly impactful pitch presentation.

You will see what great looks like, access some useful tools, and learn some highly-effective techniques and models that will help you establish solid foundations to be a successful pitch presenter.

At the end of the journey, if you feel you need to put some of this newly found knowledge and skills into action and help with reinforcement, then you can express your interest in participating in an additional face-to-face workshop. In the workshop, you will be given plenty of opportunities to practice and receive objective critique and feedback to get even better.

Click on the welcome tab to learn more about this journey and how to use it.

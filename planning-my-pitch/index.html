Planning My Pitch

In Step 3: My Value Proposition we learnt how important it was to demonstrate the value of your research, idea, or innovation to your audience. In Step 4: My Communication Style, we covered the importance of adapting my communication style and messages to the audience. In this step we will use the Visual Pitch Planner as a tool to help us to craft our messages that will demonstrate value in a way that makes sense for your audience.
